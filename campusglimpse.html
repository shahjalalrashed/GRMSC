Campus Glimpse

Government Rupnagar Model School and College is a secondary school and college located in Mirpur (Rupnagar), in the capital city of Bangladesh, Dhaka. It is one of the notable government educational institutions in the country. The institution conducts academic activities at the primary, secondary, and higher secondary levels. Currently, around 8,000 students are studying in the Bengali medium across these three levels.
